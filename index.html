Introduction

This course is designed for Physicians and appropriately supported Registrars in General Medicine, Neurology or Emergency Medicine who may be called upon to deliver stroke thrombolysis. Where possible it is recommended that cases are discussed with an on-call specialist team.

By the end of this course you should:
  • Understand the benefits and risks of thrombolysis
  • Appreciate which patients are suitable for this treatment
  • Understand the inclusion and exclusion criteria
  • Be familiar with the thrombolysis pathway and stroke scales
  • Be able to consent a patient or family member
  • Recognize and manage complications

Start with some background information before working through the suggested 8 steps pathway. You will be tested with MCQs along the way.

Please note
It is important that stroke thrombolysis is only delivered in institutions with established pathways. Please familiarise yourself with your institutions protocols before implementing the skills and knowledge covered in this program.
